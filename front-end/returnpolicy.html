RETURN POLICY

Thank you for selecting jewelry from FUJ - Please carefully review the following information to ensure the best possible support from FUJ.

FUJ offers product exchanges for customers, which fall into two categories:

1. FUJ COVERS SHIPPING EXPENSES:

  • Exchange for a new product within 7 days (from the date of receipt) in cases where the product exhibits defects from the manufacturer, such as dropped beads, discoloration from lack of use, worn rings, broken buckles, etc.
  • The product turns black, rusty, or oxidized, causing skin irritation (applies only to titanium products).
  • The product received does not match the size or color specified by the customer.
  • Missing order or incorrect product received.

(All of the above cases must be addressed within 7 days of receiving the goods; after this period, FUJ will not facilitate the exchange).

2. CUSTOMER IS RESPONSIBLE FOR SHIPPING COSTS:

If the customer is dissatisfied with the color or size of the product.

(All products in the above cases can only be resolved within 48 hours from the time you receive the goods)

Caution

  • FUJ does not handle any refund cases for customers.
  • Please return products of the same model (size, color...) unless the product is exchanged by the Customer. FUJ is out of stock and will require you to switch to another color or size.
  • If you wish to exchange for a higher-value product due to the shop's defective item, please cover the price difference. However, if the exchanged product is of lower value, FUJ will not issue a refund but require payment of the price discrepancy.
  • This policy is not applicable to promotions or gifts.
  • Product exchanges in the Earrings category are not applicable for health reasons, to avoid infectious diseases, FUJ protects all Customers.
  • Please thoroughly review the information provided and reach out to FUJ staff in advance for guidance and instructions on the return process.
